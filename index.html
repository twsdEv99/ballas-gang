ESBG

The Ballas are an African-American street gang based in Los Santos. Most of their territory can be found in the poorer run-down neighborhoods to the far east of the city. They are involved in gangbanging, gang warfare, street violence, prostitution, gang-related homicide, murder, drug trafficking manufacturing (especially the crack cocaine trade), gun-running/arms trafficking, vandalism, and degeneracy. They are easily identifiable by wearing purple clothing as their gang color.

ESBG Ballas represents the digital evolution of this notorious gang, bringing our presence to the online world through gaming, music, and content creation. We dominate the streets both virtually and in reality.
